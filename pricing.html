Frequently Asked Questions

ü§î What is a "session"?
A session is one WebRTC connection to OpenAI's Realtime API through AI-Gate. Each time your application creates a new connection, it counts as 1 session. On average, a session lasts about 2 minutes.
üí∞ What about OpenAI API costs?
AI-Gate pricing is separate from OpenAI costs. You pay OpenAI directly for their Realtime API usage using your own API key. AI-Gate only charges for the proxy infrastructure and features.
üîÑ What happens if I exceed my quota?
Once you reach your monthly session limit, new session requests will be blocked until either: (1) you upgrade to a higher plan, or (2) your quota resets at the start of the next month. You'll receive alerts at 80% and 100% usage.
‚úÖ Do failed sessions count?
No! We only count successful sessions. If a session fails due to TURN connection issues, OpenAI errors, or lasts less than 5 seconds, you won't be charged for it.
üéØ Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties or fees. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
üìä Can I see detailed usage analytics?
Yes! All plans get access to basic analytics showing session count and usage percentage. Growth plan includes advanced analytics with session duration, error rates, and custom reports.
üè¢ Do you offer enterprise plans?
Yes! For teams needing 100,000+ sessions per month, custom SLAs, or on-premise deployment, contact us at enterprise@ai-gate.dev for custom pricing.
